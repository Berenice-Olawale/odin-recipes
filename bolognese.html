Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti
  • Beef Mince
  • Onion
  • Carrot
  • Celery
  • Tinned Tomatoes
  • Tomato Puree
  • Olive oil
  • Beef stock
  • Dried Herbs

Steps To Prepare Your Meal

A traditional Spaghetti Bolognese—more accurately known in Italy as Ragù alla Bolognese—is a slow-cooked meat sauce characterized by a savory base of celery, carrots, and onions (soffritto), and the addition of milk for richness.

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Prepare the Soffritto
  2. Brown the Meat
  3. Deglaze with Wine
  4. Add Milk & Tomatoes
  5. Simmer Low and Slow
  6. Combine and Serve
